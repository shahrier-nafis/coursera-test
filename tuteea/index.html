Our Story

Our Story

We started with a single vision: to help the Word-Press community by empowering them with enterprise-level tools at a franction of the cost.

COMPANY TIMELINE
  • June,2015

    The 1st Encounter

    Emraan randomly pings Sam (7500+ miles apart) on Skype after finding him through an Envato authors group, in hopes to connect for collaboration on a new project. They chatted for a little bit and decided to work on a small project.

  • July,2015

    The New Beginning

    On 4 July, an agreement is signed to start working on HTML and WordPress theme to sell on Envato as Take-Themes.

  • August,2015

    The 1st Launch

    On 29 August, Startuprr Multi-Purpose HTML theme launched.

  • January,2016

    The 2nd Launch

    On 6 January, Startuprr LeadGen Landing Pages HTML theme launched.

  • March,2016

    The 3rd Launch

    On 26 March, ListingPro Multi-Purpose HTML theme launched.

  • June,2016

    The 4th Launch

    On 21 June, Videofy Video Portal HTML theme launched,

  • August,2016

    The 5th Launch

    On 24 August, Solution Multipurpose Small Business Html theme launched.

  • September,2016

    The 6th Launch

    On 24 September, XShop eCommerce WordPress theme launched.

  • September,2016

    Cridio Studio is Born

    On 25 September, Take-Themes becomes Cridio Studio. Everyone unite under one banner with one vision.

  • February, 2017

    Survival Instinct

    By early 2017 we had 6 product lunches but we were bleeding and struggling to survive. We had several WP products in pipeline but no focus. We decided to stay laser focused with LisitngPro to give all we got.

  • April, 2017

    Hitting Home Run

    On 20 April, ListingPro End-to-End WordPress Directory Solution launches and becomes an overnight success by making to the weekly top seller chart week after week. Rest is history.

  • July, 2017

    Joined The Elite Author's Club

    On 4 July, exactly 2 years from signing the first partership agreement our team achieved what every Envato author dreams, which is to become an Elite Author by crossing $75K in sales. Hurray! We also received a lot of cool stuff in mail.

  • August, 2017

    Elite - Level 2 - $125K Milestone

    On 22 August, we reached our next milestone, which was Level-2 as an Elite Author by crossing $125K in sales. We were buzzing with excitement as more cool stuff got delivered to us.

  • January, 2018

    Elite - Level 3 - $250K Milestone

    On 12 January, in 6 months of achieving level-2 we doubled the the numbers in sales by cross $250K and reached our next milestone. Coincidentally that is also the birthday of one of our founders. WooHoo! The cool stuff we received got better.

  • December, 2018

    Elite - Level 4 - $500K Milestone

    On 24 December, in 12 months of achieving level-3 we again doubled the the numbers in sales by cross $500K and reached our next BIG milestone. We couldn't believe we got here so fast.

  • August, 2019

    Elite - Level 5 - $750K Milestone

    On 21 August, in exactly 2 years from achieving level-2 we sextuple(That just means an increase of sixfold, and has nothing to do with what your dirty mind might think) the numbers by crossing $750K in sales and reached our next milestone which placed us one level away from reaching the legendary Power Elite status.

  • May, 2020

    PowerElite! - 1 Million Dollar Milestone

    Finally! on 5th May 2020, in just 3 years from ListingPro launch, we are grateful to achive the PowerElite title with a single product that is definetly a rare thing on Envato. We thank every member of our team and our customers who believed in us.
